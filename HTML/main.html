  Interesting Activities you can do this Janmashtami >>

  • Make your own personalised e-card on the holy occasion of janmashtami and e-mail to your family and friends.
  • Tell your child a story of Krishna's birth to teach them the significance of Gokulashtami.
  • Go in the nearest temple with your family members and perform puja and eat Prasad.
  • Enjoy the food delicacies like fruits, milk, curd and all kinds of sweets including ladoos and burfis.
  • Worship wholeheartedly for the wellbeing of yourself and your family. If you are keen on fasting, fast until midnight.
